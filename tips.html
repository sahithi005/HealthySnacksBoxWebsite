Healthy Snacking Tips

Smart snacking keeps your energy high and your cravings in check. Here are some quick and easy tips:

  • ✅ Choose snacks rich in fiber and protein like nuts, seeds, and Greek yogurt.
  • ✅ Avoid snacks with added sugars and artificial ingredients.
  • ✅ Pair carbs with protein for balanced energy (e.g., apple + peanut butter).
  • ✅ Portion your snacks to avoid overeating — use small bowls or containers.
  • ✅ Stay hydrated — thirst is often mistaken for hunger.
